    BPI DataWave 2024

    We developed an AI-driven alternative metrics model using the Isolation Forest algorithm to assess loan eligibility for MSMEs in the agricultural and fisheries sector, integrating unconventional factors such as socioeconomic status, behavioral risk analysis, and regional competitiveness to enhance financial inclusivity. Their innovative approach secured 3rd place at BPI DataWave 2024, recognizing its potential to address the limitations of traditional credit assessment models.
